Welcome to the Ghost Tour by 10ball.org

Here you will be able to play in one of several online pool tournaments. Yes this is a real life pool tournament. The tournaments will be held worldwide and can be played anywhere, anytime. The system will be readily availible at any players discretion. There will be an entry fee and payouts for any and every tournament. There will be 4 monthly tournaments and we are working on some weekly events as well. As this grows we may add more monthly tournaments, if needed.

How this works

1st. You will need to register to the site.
2nd. You will pick the tournaments that you will be playing in based on table size and preference.
3rd. You will pay buy a ticket ($20). The ticket can be used for any of our tournaments.
4th. You will set up your phone, laptop or tablet so it will be able to record your ghost match.
5th. You will be playing five racks of a modified version of the 10 ball ghost (see the rules section)


Other info

Players of any age may play, but anyone under 18 must have consent by a parent or gardian. Any player winning money will have to fill out any and all tax forms that may be applicable. The parent or gardian of a minor playing in the tournaments will be resposible for any tax form that may be applicable.
